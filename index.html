公司简介Company Profile

广东宏远集团药业有限公司 成立于1994年12月20日，前身为1991年成立的东莞市生物化学制药厂。是集研发、生产、销售于一体的国家GMP认证高新技术企业。公司坐落于东莞市东城樟村工业区，占地面积约4.2万平方米，2024年员工112人。

Guangdong Winnerway Group Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd. was founded on Dec 20, 1994. A national GMP-certified high-tech enterprise integrating R&D, production and sales. Located in Dongguan's Zhangcun Industrial Zone, covering ~42,000 m². 112 employees (2024).

🏭 集团背景

🏭 Group Background

广东宏远集团经过近二十年发展，形成以工业区开发为基础，房地产、药业为龙头，体育产业、服务业、国际贸易等多元化格局。“粤宏远”为东莞市首家上市公司（深交所），总资产22亿元。

Guangdong Winnerway Group has grown into a diversified conglomerate with industrial parks, real estate, pharmaceuticals, sports, and international trade. "Yue Hongyuan" is the first listed company in Dongguan (SZSE).

主营业务 · 产品与品牌Core Business · Products & Brands

公司专注于五大治疗领域：儿科用药、心脑血管类、抗病毒类、消化系统用药、免疫增强剂。旗下儿药品牌“果果”创立于1998年，专注儿童健康。

We focus on five therapeutic areas: Pediatric, Cardiovascular, Antiviral, Digestive, and Immune enhancers. Our pediatric brand "Guoguo" was established in 1998.

果糖二磷酸钠注射用炎琥宁辅酶Q10胶囊 小儿氨酚黄那敏注射用奥美拉唑

拥有大容量注射剂、冻干粉针、片剂、颗粒剂、硬胶囊、生化提取、中药提取、散剂及原料药等多条GMP生产线。

We have multiple GMP lines: large volume injections, lyophilized powders, tablets, granules, capsules, biochemical extraction, TCM extraction, and APIs.

集团与体育文化Group & Sports Culture

广东宏远篮球俱乐部是国内最早的职业篮球俱乐部之一，培养了易建联等多位国际篮球明星。截至2026年，宏远队共夺得11次CBA总冠军，是CBA历史上夺冠次数最多的球队，被誉为“十一冠王”。

Guangdong Winnerway Basketball Club is one of the earliest professional clubs in China, having cultivated stars like Yi Jianlian. As of 2026, the team has won 11 CBA championships—the most in league history.

集团以体育传播企业形象，形成了“以人为本，团结健康”的独特企业文化，宏远篮球已成为东莞城市文化的重要品牌。

The group promotes its image through sports, fostering a unique "people-oriented, united and healthy" culture. Winnerway Basketball is a key brand of Dongguan's city identity.
